\part{Task 2}

The rates of the nuclear reactions are shown on Figures \ref{fig_2.1_reaction_rates} - \ref{fig_2.4_reaction_rates}. We can see that the rates for reverse reactions are identical for the two different pressures. In addition, we can see that reverse reactions only happen at temperatures higher than about $\SI{1e9}{K}$, while forward reactions happen at lower temperatures.
